## **Nth Fibonacci**
Given a positive integer N. Find the Nth fibonacci number.

#### **Sample Input**
	input: N = 5

#### **Sample Output**
	A: 3

#### **Sample Explanation**
Fibonacci series: 0 1 1 2 3 5 8 13 ....
Hence 5th fibonacci is: 3

#### **Expected Time Complexity**
__O__(N)

#### **Expected Space Complexity**
__O__(1), i.e., constant space complexity.

#### **Constraints**
	1 <= N <= 100000
